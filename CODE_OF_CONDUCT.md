CODE OF CONDUCT

Our Pledge

Open Mission is committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative community for everyone.

We believe great communities are built on kindness, humility, openness, and mutual respect.

Regardless of background, experience, identity, beliefs, or skill level, everyone who participates in this project deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.

⸻

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

* Being respectful and considerate.
* Welcoming new contributors.
* Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
* Being open to different perspectives.
* Focusing on collaboration rather than competition.
* Assuming good intentions.
* Helping others learn and grow.
* Respecting community decisions.

⸻

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable within the Open Mission community:

* Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
* Personal attacks or insulting language.
* Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
* Publishing private information without permission.
* Threats or intimidation.
* Any behavior that creates an unsafe or hostile environment.

⸻

Community Philosophy

Open Mission exists to build open knowledge, open data, and open technology through collaboration.

Healthy discussions are encouraged.

Disagreements are natural, but they should always remain respectful and focused on ideas rather than individuals.

⸻

Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, discussions, or other contributions that violate these guidelines.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the project.

⸻

Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers privately.

Every report will be handled respectfully and, whenever possible, confidentially.

⸻

Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Open Mission spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories
* GitHub Discussions
* Issues
* Pull Requests
* Community chats
* Events organized by the project

⸻

Our Commitment

We are building more than software.

We are building a community where people from around the world can collaborate to make biblical resources more accessible through openness, respect, and shared purpose.
